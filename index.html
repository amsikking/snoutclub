SnoutClub: Home page

Purpose

To create a supportive community of microscope builders and developers who have an AMS-AGY objective (a.k.a. #Snouty or #KingSnout).

Are you looking to contact someone that has built a single-objective light-sheet or similar microscope?

Check out this list!

How to join

Publish, post or announce online if you have a #Snouty!

Updates

Make a pull request to this repository or email me: amsikking+snoutclub@gmail.com

(please include a link to your public announcement)
